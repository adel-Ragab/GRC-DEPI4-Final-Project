# 🚗 GRC-DEPI4-Final-Project

## 📌 Project Title

**GRC Role in attracting and sustainability of business to Egypt.**

---

## 🧾 Project Overview

This repository contains the final graduation project for the **DEPI GRC Round 4** track.

The project simulates a consultancy engagement for a German international car manufacturer planning to establish a factory and management office in Egypt.

The main objective is to assess the business feasibility of the expansion and design a practical **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)** framework for the Egyptian operation.

The project focuses on business feasibility, IT governance, risk management, ISO 27001 extension, GDPR/PDPL compliance, policies, technical controls, and audit readiness.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are to:

- ✅ Assess the feasibility of establishing a German car manufacturing operation in Egypt.
- ✅ Design a practical IT governance framework for the Egypt office.
- ✅ Ensure alignment with the mother company’s governance and security policies.
- ✅ Define clear roles, responsibilities, committees, and reporting lines.
- ✅ Build a structured risk register and risk treatment plan.
- ✅ Support ISO 27001 extension to Egypt’s data center.
- ✅ Address GDPR and Egyptian PDPL compliance requirements.
- ✅ Prepare the organization for internal and external audits.

---

## 🧩 Project Scope

The project is divided into two main parts:

---

## 📊 Part I: Business Feasibility Study

This part evaluates Egypt as a potential location for establishing a German car manufacturing facility.

### Main areas covered:

- 📈 Market analysis
- 💰 Financial analysis
- 🚚 Supply chain and logistics
- ⚖️ Laws and regulations
- 👥 Labor and human resources
- 🌍 Economic and operational risks
- 🌱 Environmental impact
- 🤝 Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
- 🖥️ Digital transformation roadmap
- 🔄 Change management plan

---

## 🏛️ Part II: IT Governance Framework

This part designs a complete IT governance framework for the Egyptian management office and data center.

### Main areas covered:

- 🏢 Governance structure
- 🔗 Reporting lines between Egypt and the mother company
- 👤 Roles and responsibilities
- 📋 RACI matrix
- ⚠️ Risk register
- 🛡️ Risk treatment plan
- 🔐 ISO 27001 extension plan
- 🇪🇺 GDPR compliance
- 🇪🇬 Egyptian PDPL compliance
- 📚 Policies, procedures, and standards
- 🧰 Technical controls
- 🔎 Audit and monitoring readiness

---

## 🏛️ Governance Focus

The governance model includes:

- 🧭 IT Steering Committee
- 🛡️ Security & Risk Committee
- 🔎 Audit & Compliance Committee
- 👨‍💼 Group CIO responsibilities
- 🔐 Group CISO responsibilities
- 🇪🇬 Egypt IT Manager responsibilities
- ⚠️ Risk Management responsibilities
- 🧾 Internal Audit responsibilities
- 📢 Escalation and reporting lines

---

## ⚠️ Risk Management Focus

The project includes risk management deliverables covering:

- 🔍 Risk identification
- 📊 Risk assessment
- 📈 Risk analysis
- ✅ Risk evaluation
- 🛡️ Risk treatment planning
- 📌 Residual risk acceptance
- 🔄 Risk monitoring and review

## 🔐 Compliance Focus

The project addresses compliance requirements related to:

- ✅ ISO 27001

- ✅ GDPR
