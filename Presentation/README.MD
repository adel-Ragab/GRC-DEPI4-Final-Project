# 🎤 Presentation

## 📌 Purpose

This folder contains the final presentation materials for the **GRC-DEPI4 Final Project**.

The presentation summarizes the key outcomes of the project, including the business feasibility study, IT governance framework, risk management approach, ISO 27001 extension plan, GDPR/PDPL compliance, and audit readiness.

---

## 🚗 Project Title

**Feasibility Study and IT Governance Framework for a German Car Manufacturer Expanding into Egypt**

---

## 📊 Presentation Scope

The presentation should provide an executive-level summary of the project and cover the following areas:

- 🧾 Project overview
- 🌍 Business feasibility study
- 🏛️ IT governance framework
- 👥 Governance committees and reporting lines
- 📋 Roles and responsibilities
- ⚠️ Risk register summary
- 🛡️ Risk treatment plan
- 🔐 ISO 27001 extension plan
- ⚖️ GDPR and Egyptian PDPL compliance
- 📚 Policies, procedures, and standards
- 🧰 Technical controls
- 🔎 Audit and monitoring readiness
- ✅ Final recommendations

---

## 🧩 Suggested Slide Structure

| # | Slide Title | Description |
|---|------------|-------------|
| 1 | 🚗 Project Title | Project name, team members, and program details. |
| 2 | 🧾 Executive Summary | Brief overview of the business case and GRC objectives. |
| 3 | 🌍 Business Feasibility Overview | Summary of market, financial, legal, labor, and operational feasibility. |
| 4 | 🏛️ IT Governance Model | Governance structure for the Egypt office and alignment with the mother company. |
| 5 | 👥 Committees & Reporting Lines | IT Steering Committee, Security & Risk Committee, and Audit & Compliance Committee. |
| 6 | 📋 Roles & Responsibilities | Group CIO, Group CISO, Egypt IT Manager, Risk, Compliance, and Audit roles. |
| 7 | ⚠️ Risk Register Summary | Key strategic, operational, compliance, and IT risks. |
| 8 | 🛡️ Risk Treatment Plan | Risk responses, mitigation actions, owners, and residual risk. |
| 9 | 🔐 ISO 27001 Extension | Scope, risk assessment, controls, evidence, and audit readiness. |
| 10 | ⚖️ GDPR & PDPL Compliance | Data protection requirements and compliance approach. |
| 11 | 📚 Policies & Procedures | Required governance, security, privacy, and operational policies. |
| 12 | 🧰 Technical Controls | Access control, encryption, monitoring, backup, and disaster recovery. |
| 13 | 🔎 Audit & Monitoring | Internal audit, external audit readiness, and continuous compliance monitoring. |
| 14 | ✅ Final Recommendations | Key recommendations and next steps. |
| 15 | 🙏 Thank You | Closing slide and Q&A. |
